Fennec Fox

Fennex foxes' extraordinary big ears are so sensitive that they can hear prey moving underground.

  • Scientific Name: Vulpes zerda
  • Average Lifespan: 11 years
  • Average Length: 9.4 - 16 in.
  • Habitat: Sandy Deserts (North Africa)

The fennec fox is a small crepuscular fox native to the Sahara Desert and the Sinai Peninsula. Its most distinctive feature is its unusually large ears, which serve to dissipate heat. The fennec is the smallest canid species.
